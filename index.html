About Kor


Korblox is a new platform to share your games! You use the Korblox Editor to make your games and publish them to our platform. You can monetize your game for free and play other users games. The currency is KorPoints.

How you make your dreams come to life is up to you! Join our program and get 500 free KorPoints

Korblox staff and moderation team takes extra steps to insure kids can play the platform. Messages are limited to only 13+ and payment options must be filled out by a guardian if under 18 to avoid incidents.

To signup, click the Signup button on the left of the screen. By signing up you agree to the terms of service!
